## Naming Criteria

The name should **NOT** imply:
- Gambling or betting with real stakes
- Trading or financial products
- Casual "guess and forget" games

The name **SHOULD** convey:
- Long-term track record / reputation
- Accuracy measurement
- Credibility through results
- Playful but consequential
